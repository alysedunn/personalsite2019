Novice Newcommer to Professional Programmer

9Feb- 2019

I am a self taught software engineer. By that, I mean I don't have a degree in computer science or anything close to it, and I learned how to code without any formal education. As such, the most common question that I get in my professional career is: 'How did you do it? How did you transition from a completely non-technical role, into a full-time software engineering role?'

I get this question so often that it occurred to me that there's likely a lot of demand for information about how people can make the transition into such a sought-after role when they don't have any formal training in it. So, I posed this very question (and a few others) to a few self-taught engineers who I know, and I share their responses here for the curious observer.
